Article Title

One of the greatest and most endearing things about technology is that it’s always moving forward. This constant and progressive clip means that tasks that were once prohibitively difficult are now simple and easy to manage. Just think about it – a mere sixty years ago, people were washing their clothes by hand!

In the last five years, the convenience economy has exploded to make things like grocery shopping or getting a ride to the airport easier than ever before. What’s interesting about the way we interact with technology is that people are often more open to acting as an early adopter of technology in their personal lives than they are in their professional lives.

A New Era

Take sales tracking software for example; while it has the capability to completely revolutionize sales departments, many companies are used to the status quo and are still tracking sales progress and commissions on spreadsheets.

Luckily for today’s businesses, the popularity and usage of an advanced solution is on the rise. You might be wondering why I’m using the word ‘luckily’, and I’m prepared to tell you. Just as the washing machine saved people the incredible amount of time and effort that they used to spent on this household chore, sales tracking tools that replace outdated means of tracking, analyzing, and sharing sales data saves people hours, days, and even weeks of tedious, manual work.

The Benefits of Sales Tracking Software

Today, people that are using a manual process instead of software to compensate their reps are putting in as much as two extra weeks’ worth of work just for calculating commissions. This doesn’t even factor in the time it takes to design the plans or communicate them to the reps. The same companies also leave themselves vulnerable to risk and errors. Without an automated solution to mitigate human error, spreadsheets are subject to a 3-10 percent error rate, according to Gartner.

From plan design and calculations to reporting and analytics, sales tracking software makes even the most complicated compensation program easy to manage and error free. Sales tracking software has numerous capabilities that spreadsheets, even on their best day, will never have. Welcome to a new era – where you can test payouts under various performance scenarios to understand their financial impact before they affect your bottom line.

One of the most beneficial aspects of a tracking solution is the increased visibility for the entire company – every department that currently contributes to the sales compensation process benefits from sales tracking software. Sales Operations no longer has to spend overtime hours checking and re-checking their math for incentive compensation payouts, Finance no longer has to wonder if human error is going to throw off their numbers or lead to inaccurate forecasts, and Sales won’t be surprised by the team’s results near the end of the quarter because when leaders use sales tracking software, they have complete real-time visibility into sales progress.

Who Benefits the Most?

Sales tracking software isn’t just great for people managing and optimizing sales; it’s a game-changer for reps that are out in the field doing the actual selling. When they have confidence that every deal they close is accounted for, they spend less time calculating their own commissions or checking them for accuracy, and more time creating relationships with prospective customers. Automation ensures that sales reps are in the know about how much they are going to make on each deal, and how much they could potentially make if they hit it out of the park.

An automated tracking platform is essential for any company that wants to take control of the entire sales process end-to-end. From planning and design, to motivating sales people and analyzing results, it’s essential to bring your business into to the future of sales compensation with sales tracking software. Drop the manual processes quicker than your grandma dropped hand-washing her family’s laundry for the newest Whirlpool.
